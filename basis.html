ONE PEICE

ONE PEICE is written by Eiichiro oda
It has been serialized in Shueisha's shōnen manga magazine Weekly Shōnen Jump since July 1997,
with its individual chapters compiled into 105 tankōbon volumes as of March 2023. The story follows the adventures of Monkey D. Luffy
and his crew, the Straw Hat Pirates, where he explores the Grand Line in search of the mythical treasure known as the "One Piece"
in order to become the next King of the Pirates.
